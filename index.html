Persephone

Goddess of Spring, Queen of the underworld

My mother is Demeter and I am the Goddess of spring. I love hyacinths, roses, and dahlias.
I live in a beautiful gothic castle in Tartarus.
I have a garden of black flowers that I tend too.
My crown is made up of blackened rose thornes, a few roses and finger bones.
